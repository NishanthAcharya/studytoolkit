Step-by-step guide on how to use a zip file on your mobile device:

  1. Click on the download button for your desired subject.
  2. You'll be redirected to the Google Drive download box.
  3. At the top of the box, locate and click on the download button.
  4. The download will initiate automatically, saving a zip file containing the study materials to your file manager.
  5. If you haven't already, download the ZArchiver or RAR app from the Play Store or App Store.
  6. Open the ZArchiver app, navigate to the search bar, and enter the name of the zip file (e.g., Kannada.rar).
  7. Select the file and choose the option to extract it.
  8. Choose an empty folder for extraction.
  9. Once the zip file is extracted, you can access the individual PDF files.
